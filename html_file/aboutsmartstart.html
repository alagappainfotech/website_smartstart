Welcome to Smart Start Play School And Daycare Center

At Smart Start, we provide a safe, nurturing space where your child's learning and growth begin. Our play-based programs focus on literacy, numeracy, creativity, and social skills. Experienced educators give personalized attention to meet each child's unique needs.

Daycare

Our daycare services are designed to provide a home away from home,where your child can flourish in a safe, nurturing, and joyful environment. Choose Smart Start and give your child a head start for a bright, confident future.

Nursery

At Smart Start Playschool, our Nursery program is designed to make your child’s first learning experience fun, safe, and exciting. With a focus on play-based activities, we help children explore, learn, and grow through creative arts, stories, games, and group play.

Junior Kindergarten

our Junior KG program is all about making learning fun and exciting while building a strong foundation for your child’s future. Through creative activities, interactive games, and hands-on lessons, children develop essential skills in reading, writing, numbers, and social interactions. Our experienced and caring teachers create a safe and supportive environment where your child feels confident, curious, and ready to explore new things.

Senior Kindergarten

Our Senior KG program prepares your child for primary school with fun activities, hands-on learning, and group play. We focus on building skills in language, math, social interaction, and problem-solving, helping children gain confidence and independence for the next educational step.
